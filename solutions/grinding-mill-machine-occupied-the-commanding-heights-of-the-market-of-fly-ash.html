Grinding mill machine occupied the commanding heights of the market of fly ash

This project is based on the "three wastes" of fly ash as raw material, made of superfine fiber, and then the manufacture of pulp, paper products (mainly for the production of packaging paper products) and insulation materials, energy-saving wall materials etc.. The characteristics of the project for the use of renewable resources, turning waste into treasure, which belongs to the field of environmental protection project, high technology content, strong innovation, project of great significance. Fly ash waste resources are very abundant in China, the use of fly ash production of paper products, packaging paper products market is particularly good because of the raw material; structure and environmental effect, reduce the cost of fly ash paper up to 50% (varies according to different product requirements and the structure of raw materials), profit and high added value; the superfine fly ash fiber preparation, papermaking waste recycling, waste recycling or degradation, green, harmony with the environment, no pollution to the environment; compared with the traditional papermaking technology, fly ash wood pulp paper saving water, reducing wastewater discharge. This technology is a revolution of papermaking raw material structure adjustment, can create significant economic benefits, environmental benefits and social benefits. The new technology is the first at home and abroad, has 3 invention patents, with independent intellectual property rights. Typical demonstration project in 2005 June in Xiamen Yung Hing Paper Manufacturing Co., Ltd. to achieve industrial production.

The technical content of industrialization including fly ash to make follow-up superfine fiber superfine fiber, pulp made two parts, or subsequent production insulation materials, wall materials. Preparation of superfine fly ash fiber is the fly ash as raw material and packing the appropriate formula, the parameters into the melting temperature, viscosity, fiber furnace melt crystallization degree and production equipment, production of diameter, fiber length, strength and toughness, slag ball content reached the technical requirements of the paper fiber, and then using the technology and the appropriate equipment for deep processing of crude fiber (physical and geometric mean of slag) and chemical modification (softening and fiber surface properties as appropriate) and slag processing, made of dry pulp, sent to the mill with wood fiber pulp, copy paper product. Fly ash is cheap, mining, crushing, transportation, material costs less than 70 yuan / ton, other mineral materials is about 70 yuan / ton, fly ash fiber transformation rate from 75 to 90%; the project cost per ton of bare fiber of 600 to 800 yuan. According to the purpose and grade of paper products, pulp superfine fiber production cost 1000 to 1400 yuan. The fly ash fiber pulp and 60-70% pulp, straw pulp and waste paper pulp mixed into a slurry, production of inorganic fiber papermaking specialty paper, on the basis of the difference between how much quantity and paper products, can save more than 10% of the cost of paper making. Fly ash fiber papermaking paper with fire, temperature resistance, corrosion resistance, little hygroscopicity, moth and other unique advantages, the product of the project as a special paper, color retention, flexibility and lasting stability, surface properties of light, no radiation hazard. High temperature resistant plant fiber paper at 400-450 DEG C, and the fly ash fiber papermaking paper temperature up to 650-700 degrees, so it has good fire performance.

The advantages of the traditional plant fiber paper do not have, so the products have strong competitive product performance. According to the Ministry of light industry forecast to 2010 Chinese market for paper and paperboard consumption of about 5000 tons to 60000000 tons, 40 kilograms per capita / year. The project is a multi-disciplinary field is intensive, high-tech projects, the technology to fill the domestic blank, at the international leading level. The formula of raw materials, chemical fiber production projects in the softening of modifying agent, papermaking additives and papermaking technology and equipment technology to protect 5-8 and unique in the market, with technology advantage and technical products exclusive possession of competitiveness. With an annual output of 20000 tons of superfine fiber and subsequent pulp manufacturing equipment (or subsequent heat preservation material manufacturing equipment), including the transfer of technology, investment cost of about 20000000 yuan, take a turn key project mode.
