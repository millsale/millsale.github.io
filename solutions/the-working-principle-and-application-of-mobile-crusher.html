The working principle and application of mobile crusher

Mobile crusher is the Shanghai shibang launched a series of rock and construction waste crushing equipment, greatly expanding the coarse crushing, fine crushing concept field. The purpose of its design concept is stand in the position of customers, to eliminate the broken ground, environment, complicated configuration and complex logistics to customer breaking barriers, as the primary solution. In the new concept of "close to" principle of material. The real operation of the project of hardware facilities is simple, efficient and low cost for the customer. According to the requirements of the crushing process composed of different "first broken screen, can also be composed of" screening and crushing process, crushing station can be according to the actual demand for coarse crushing and fine crushing and screening system two, can also be combined into the thick and thin three stage screening system has high flexibility, can maximum limit to meet the needs of different customers. My company production of mobile crushing station set feeding, crushing, conveying process as a whole, by optimizing the process so that it has excellent rock crushing, aggregate production, open pit mining crushing performance, through the combination of different models, form a powerful crushing production line processing completed demand. Its advanced design, excellent performance, high production efficiency, convenient operation and maintenance, operation cost, stable and reliable.

Mobile crusher in accordance with customer requirements into standard type and closed type, as well as the expansion of multi combination according to the requirements of the launch of high-end customers, to the maximum to meet customer demand. The mobile crusher is widely used in the construction of Luqiao City, building, metallurgy, energy, and other departments, crushing, screening and other operations. Especially for the flow of highway, railway, hydropower engineering rock and other operations, the user can according to the type of material, size and material requirements are different with different configurations. City construction waste treatment process according to the policies and the actual environment is different, the process can be divided into: centralized and decentralized approach in two ways. The so-called centralized processing, refers to the building garbage to a specific location, sorting and processing of unity. According to the market situation reflected by the customer, can complete selection of crushing equipment fixed, mobile crushing station equipment can also be combined to choose convenient transfer. For construction waste accumulation is not too concentrated, or be divided into several regions to deal with the situation, in order to reduce the equipment repeat purchase, save the equipment cost considerations, the most suitable for the mobile crushing and processing equipment, the new mobile crushing station combination equipment.
