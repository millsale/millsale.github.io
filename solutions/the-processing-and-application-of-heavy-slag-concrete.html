The processing and application of heavy slag concrete

Heavy slag: air-cooled blast furnace slag or by pouring hot water spray after treatment was called slag blast heavy slag, slag re-referred. After heavy slag crushing and screening derived 4.75mm diameter larger than the weight of slag particles, called heavy slag gravel, crushed slag concrete used to make the weight, can be used in general industrial and civil buildings and structures design strength grade C50 and C50 The following concrete, reinforced concrete and prestressed concrete. Slag and gravel production process there are two kinds of methods dike spilled hot method. After the blast furnace slag heavy natural cooling or water spray cooling in the slag or slag pit floor designated to form a more dense slag through mining, crushing, magnetic separation and screening to obtain a gravel-like material.
