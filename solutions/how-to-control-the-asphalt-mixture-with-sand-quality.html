How to control the asphalt mixture with sand quality

The quality of the relationship with the mechanism of sand asphalt mixture quality. Asphalt mixture as the pavement of asphalt mixture, so the more stringent requirements. Factors influencing the quality of asphalt mixture are the quality of raw materials, mixing process and the influence of transport process. The influence of raw material is more important, the coarse aggregate in the soft rock and flaky particles will have a greater impact on the quality of the fine aggregate mixture, including stone, artificial sand and natural sand, stone chips are by-products of stone crushing process, its strength is low, the general should be cautious the use of artificial sand road, is the most ideal of fine aggregate, sand often belong to acidic stone and asphalt, poor adhesion, and other fine aggregate with the use of.
