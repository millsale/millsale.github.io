Pebbles sand making production line project

Shanghai Shibang take you into the Hunan Huaihua sometime produces 600 tons of pebbles sand production line project site. The sand stone production line by the Shanghai state of the world the whole design, including project consulting, material testing machine, production line design, installation and commissioning, worker training, smooth start. At present, has been successfully put into operation, and highly praised by the owners. The sand production line production sand gradation, grain shape, containing powder process index is excellent, and the performance is stable, can be controlled, can be comparable with the natural sand with high quality, good particle shape, which are 2.36-4.75mm, 1.18-2.36mm, 0.6-1.18mm, fineness modulus can be adjusted between 2.2-3.2. Its products mainly supply the concrete mixing station. The overall configuration of the production 600 tons of sand production line process design to the whole production line to dry process, the most reasonable, the maximum extent of natural sand in the formation of natural fracture, natural washing effect, which greatly enhance the performance of the finished product of sand.

The main production line configuration process: The first stage: coarse crushing; Large stone by silo by vibrating feeder uniform feeding, conveying to the jaw crusher for crushing, crushed pebble sieving shaker, transported by belt conveyor to a crusher and cone crusher for crushing. The second stage: the crushing; Delivery to the back breaking / cone crusher in crushing stone after, using circular vibration screen screening, larger particles of pebbles returned to counterattack broken / cone crusher broken again. The third stage: Sand; The crushed pebble into the sand making machine for further broken plastic, such as the need to clean the pebbles, sand washing machine cleaning can be sent to. The fourth stage: packaging;The dried products of gravel packing and storage. It is worth mentioning that, Shanghai sebang aggregate system can be very good to be sand grading in grade two zone, under the same cement concrete, sebang VU aggregate optimization system of the mechanism of sand production can get higher strength, greatly meet the needs of dry mixed mortar in concrete and sand the.
