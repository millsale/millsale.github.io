Three methods of construction waste disposal

The use of temporary backfill, piling and landfill disposal. The use of construction waste backfill is mainly used for ground leveling, roadbed filling, depression. For site preparation, road subgrade construction waste crushing backfill should be based on the use of requirements, for depression filling construction waste crushing backfill directly without using the. Construction waste storage yard by suburban town of low-lying land or valley, the conditions are ripe, the construction waste resource utilization or transported to the landfill disposal. Construction waste storage yard should be located in convenient transportation, construction waste generated from near the source is not the recent planning, use, storage capacity to meet the requirements of the temporary stacking area. Construction waste landfill site can be city, county as a unit set. Construction waste landfill in low-lying areas, should choose natural Valley (AO), waste pit quarry and convenient transportation, land use reasonable distance, low value, water poor area; landfill capacity should be guaranteed within the service area of damaged building garbage and reconstruction of the building garbage landfill. Construction waste are renewable resources, including waste residue, waste concrete, waste brick, wood, waste steel, waste metal component. The disaster area construction waste utilization should be given priority to with simple, practical and backfill regeneration utilization. Reuse of the component, structure, damage to protect the value of the ancient architecture and traditional dwellings maintenance component, especially the decorative elements, according to the original process, the original function is applied to the reconstruction of the building (structure) to build the original position.

Due to the construction of garbage is relatively concentrated, space is limited, and the traffic is not very convenient, suggested the use of mobile crushing station. First of all the equipment for the combination of mobile crushing and screening, multi-stage crushing of various kinds of large bulk material. In addition to the mobile station equipment, crushing equipment covers an area of small, flexible, convenient, strong maneuverability, can save a lot of infrastructure and relocation costs; can carry on to the material field breaking instead of material transported from the scene and then broken, and with the raw material mining face advancing mobile, thus greatly reducing the transportation cost of the material. Flexible configuration of mobile crushing station, according to the actual site design or special mobile crushing station, very suitable for construction waste crushing.
