Stone factory according to correct selection of suitable material crusher? In the crushing process, the key configuration ore crushing production line is the ore properties, hardness, fineness, the factors are very important to the crusher is chosen. According to different fineness of hardness, material selection, crushing equipment also has the very big difference. The relative size of coarse aggregate, due to micro cracks between the more, the hardness of a material is relatively small, this stone is easily broken; and for the relatively small size of the material, the hardness of the material is relatively large, will be difficult to break. In a word, crusher in crushing material in the process because the material crushing the grain size decreased, efficiency will be decreased, the relative energy consumption will increase, and the particle size of material is fine, it resists breaking ability will be stronger. The hardness is ground, is the remnant of the hardness of the phenomenon known as selective crushing. So according to the actual situation, choose the suitable crushing equipment.
