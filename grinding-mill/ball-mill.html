Ball Mill

Energy saving ball mill pulverizing system as an indispensable auxiliary system of coal-fired power plant safe and economic operation of the unit, it directly affects the safety and economy of power plant operation. Among them, the ball mill coal pulverizing system as an important part of, has been widely used in thermal power plant, has the advantages of reliable operation, easy maintenance, strong adaptability, low maintenance cost. Energy saving control of ball mill coal pulverizing system has the following three requirements. 1, ensure that the amount of coal mill in the near optimal coal storage, theoretical analysis and practical experience show that the number of ball mill, energy consumption and production of powder basically has nothing to do, as much as possible to make coal, coal storage in ball mill should be as close as possible to the best coal storage capacity, which is the key to improve the economic stability system. Ball mill coal storage is generally controlled by adjustment of coal. 2, in order to improve the drying capacity of the mill, should try to improve the drying temperature, but in order to prevent the coal dust explosion sent the ball mill, and shall ensure that the ball mill outlet temperature does not exceed the specified value.
